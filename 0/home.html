Welcome to the CBN Insomnia
Research Study

We invite you to participate in an exciting new research study that involves testing a new medication that may help improve sleep and daytime function in people with chronic insomnia.

You may be eligible to participate in this study if you:

Live in New South Wales
Are aged 25 to 60 years
Have chronic insomnia (persistent problems falling or staying asleep)

This study will test the effects of two doses of a cannabis-based medicine on sleep and daytime function in people with chronic insomnia.

If eligible, you will need to come into the Woolcock Institute of Medical Research in Glebe, Sydney and stay in one of our overnight sleep suites on four occasions. We will measure various aspects of your sleep and cognition over an 18-hour period. You will receive reimbursement for your time and, at no expense to you, study medication and study-related evaluations from a doctor.

Your participation in this study is voluntary.
